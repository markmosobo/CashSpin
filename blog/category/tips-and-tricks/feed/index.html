Inside bets

In a typical roulette game, when you look at the basic table layout, you will notice that the main part of the table is made up of numbers which start from 0 -36 while the rest are sectors for waging on groups of numbers. Outside bets

These bets are those that cover bigger groups of numbers are a further sub-divided into 5 types. Red or black are divisions on the basis of color , odd or even are on the basis of whether the outcome is odd or even while dozens means one ought to bet on either of three dozens on the layout table. The column bet involves choosing which column will win while (1-18) or (19-36) involves choosing whether the winning number will be lower or higher than 19.

Outside bet

In this bet type, numbers 1,5,9,12,14, 16,19,23,27,30, 32 and 34 create the shape of a snake and make it one bet type while numbers 2, 6, 8, 10, 13, 17, 20, 24, 26, 29, 28 and 35 make another snake belt and are considered another bet type (black snake). 17/20 numbers and 26/29 are split bets and the other bets are straight ones.
